## Statistics in Use

### 1. "76% of local searches end in a visit within 24 hours"

- **Source:** Google / IPSOS, *"Understanding Consumers' Local Search Behavior"*, May 2014
- **URL:** https://www.thinkwithgoogle.com/consumer-insights/consumer-trends/local-search-behavior/
- **Context:** Refers specifically to smartphone local searches. The stat has been cited by Google in marketing materials consistently since 2014.
- **Site locations:** index.html (problem section header), how-it-works.html (stat grid)
- **Displayed attribution:** [Google/IPSOS, 2014]
- **Risk:** Study is from 2014. Behavior patterns may have shifted. Consider updating with BrightLocal 2023 data if a fresher source is preferred.

---

### 2. "Businesses that respond to reviews earn 35% more revenue"

- **Source:** Womply Research, *"How online reviews impact local business"*, 2019
- **URL:** https://womply.com/impact-of-online-reviews-on-local-revenue/
- **Context:** Womply analyzed data from 200,000+ small businesses. They found businesses that respond to more than 25% of their reviews average 35% more revenue than businesses that don't respond at all.
- **Site locations:** index.html (problem card amber callout)
- **Displayed attribution:** [Womply Research, 2019]
- **Risk:** Correlation, not causation. Businesses that respond may be more operationally engaged overall. Claim is accurate as stated but nuance should be acknowledged internally.

---

### 3. "Active profiles get 5× more views than inactive ones"

- **Source:** Google My Business (now Google Business Profile) Insights data, widely published 2019–2021
- **URL:** Multiple references in Google Help Center and Think with Google; exact primary URL unavailable — derived from aggregate GBP Insights data.
- **Context:** Google has published that complete, active business profiles receive significantly more visibility. The 5× figure has been cited in Google's own SMB materials.
- **Site locations:** index.html (problem card amber callout), how-it-works.html (stat grid)
- **Displayed attribution:** [Google My Business Insights, 2019]
- **Risk:** Exact primary source URL is not directly linkable. Consider replacing with BrightLocal or Moz data if a citable URL is required. Alternative source: BrightLocal Local SEO Industry Survey.

---

### 4. "73% of local searches end in a Map Pack click"

- **Source:** BrightLocal, *Local Consumer Review Survey* / *Local SEO Industry Survey*, 2023
- **URL:** https://www.brightlocal.com/research/
- **Context:** BrightLocal tracks click behavior on local search results. The Map Pack (3-pack) dominates local click share.
- **Site locations:** index.html (GBP commitment section stat grid)
- **Displayed attribution:** [BrightLocal, 2023]
- **Risk:** Percentage varies by query type and device. This is a reasonable ballpark figure. Verify against the specific BrightLocal 2023 publication.

---

### 5. "56% higher conversion with an active profile"

- **Source:** Google Business Profile Help / Google Internal Data, 2022
- **URL:** https://support.google.com/business/answer/10515606
- **Context:** Google has published data showing verified, complete profiles see meaningfully higher conversion (calls, directions, website clicks) vs. sparse/unclaimed profiles.
- **Site locations:** index.html (GBP commitment section stat grid)
- **Displayed attribution:** [Google Business Profile Data, 2022]
- **Risk:** The specific 56% figure should be verified against the most current Google support documentation. If unavailable, consider replacing with "2× more likely to be considered reputable" (a more commonly cited Google claim).

---

### 6. "88% of consumers trust online reviews as much as a personal recommendation"

- **Source:** BrightLocal, *Local Consumer Review Survey*, 2023
- **URL:** https://www.brightlocal.com/research/local-consumer-review-survey/
- **Context:** BrightLocal's annual survey is the most widely cited source for consumer review behavior. The 88% figure has appeared in various editions (ranging from 84–91% across years).
- **Site locations:** how-it-works.html (stat grid)
- **Displayed attribution:** [BrightLocal Consumer Review Survey, 2023]
- **Risk:** Low. BrightLocal is a reputable, annually updated source. Verify the specific percentage against the current 2023/2024 edition.
