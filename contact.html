Common questions before contacting us

Do you offer phone support?

No phone calls, no hold music. We handle all support by email. This isn't a cost-cutting measure — it's intentional. Email creates a clear record of every request, every response, and every decision. Reputation and Authority plan clients get a 12-hour first-response SLA.

I'm an existing client. How do I reach you?

Email support@localboostlab.com with your business name in the subject line. Include your GBP URL if your request is profile-specific. We prioritize client emails and respond within 24 hours — usually much faster.

I want to cancel. What do I do?

Email us at support@localboostlab.com with "Cancel" in the subject. We'll deactivate your account within 24 hours. No questions asked, no retention call, no hoops. You own all your GBP data — we won't touch your profile after cancellation.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. Email support@localboostlab.com and we'll process the change at your next billing cycle. Upgrades take effect immediately. There's no fee to change plans and no contract locking you into a tier.
