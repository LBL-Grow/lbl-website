What Happens After You Onboard

This is what runs in the background while you're running your business.

Review Management

Every review, handled within minutes.

Our system monitors your Google Business Profile 24/7. The moment a new review appears:

  • 4–5 star reviews: Auto-responded in your voice in under 1 hour
  • 3-star reviews: You get an email alert with a draft response within 15 minutes. You approve or edit.
  • 1–2 star reviews: Urgent alert within 5 minutes. We draft a response but never post without your explicit approval

✓ We NEVER auto-respond to anything below 4 stars.

Content Publishing

Three posts a week. Every week. Without you.

Every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at 9 AM your local time, a new post goes live on your Google Business Profile.

Each post is:

  • Written specifically for your business and services
  • Optimized for local SEO keywords in your area
  • Using your photos (when available) or AI-generated images
  • Never repeating the same topic within 30 days
  • Formatted for maximum GBP engagement (proper length, CTA, hashtags)

Posts may include AI-generated images when client photos are unavailable. All written content is AI-generated and reviewed for accuracy before publishing.
