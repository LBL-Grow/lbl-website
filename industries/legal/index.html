What LBL does for law firms

Professional review management, compliant educational content, and monthly performance reporting — done for you.

Professional Review Responses

Every 4 and 5-star review gets a measured, empathetic, professional reply within 1 hour. We never confirm or discuss case details. Negative reviews — which carry exceptional weight in legal — come to you for approval first. Every response signals the professionalism your prospective clients are screening for.

Educational GBP Posts · Bar-Compliant

We post Monday, Wednesday, Friday at 9 AM. Content focuses on consumer rights, what to expect from specific legal processes, and practice-area education — never outcome claims, never promise language, never anything that could trigger a bar complaint. Topics never repeat within 30 days.

Monthly Performance Reports

On the 5th of each month: review trends, post engagement, profile views, call and website-click volume. Two-minute read. We highlight the signals that matter for a professional services firm — not just vanity metrics.

Profile Monitoring & Accuracy

Practice area categorization matters enormously for legal GBP visibility. We monitor for unauthorized edits and keep your service categories, hours, and contact details accurate and complete — so you appear in the right searches for the right practice areas.
